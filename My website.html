Course Information

Basic Programming: You’ll perform the role of a developer, learning the basics of programming in HTML, CSS and even some JavaScript. You certainly won’t be measured as full time coders by the end of the course, but you’ll have enough to get started and give you hands on experience of life as a programmer.
Life in a Tech team: We’ll cover Agile from its founding principles as well as best practices relating to the delivery of tech projects. This will include the practical day-to-day processes of Scrum as well as a deeper understanding of how requirements are created, investigated, developed, tested and verified as completed in tech projects.
Testing: Arguably the most important part of Software Development. What are all the ways we test a product? Why are we testing? How can we check that our tests are adding value?
Deployment/Maintenance: OK so we’ve got the requirements, built the product, tested it works in isolation. But how do we make it accessible to users? Where does the product go, and how do we make sure it keeps working?
